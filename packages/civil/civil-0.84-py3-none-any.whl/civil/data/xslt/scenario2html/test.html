Clash in the pass Game Info
Number of turns:30
Battlefield Location:Littletown
Battle Date/Time:18/6/1863 — 11:30

Overview:

Willy's Junction is an important junction in the road going from Littletown and westwards. The side who controls that junction also controls several important supply roads.

Confederate Mission:

Rush to Willy's Junction and hold it at all cost. If you lose that junction you have also made it very hard to deliver supplies to Bracken's army in the north.

Union Mission:

Your mission is to capture Willy's Junction from the confederates and hold it. You can expect that the confederates will not give it up without a fight. Capturing the road junction could mean a hard blow to the conferedate supply situation.
